# Support

Questions, ideas, and "is this a bug?" are all welcome.

- **Something's wrong, or a feature request** — open an issue using the
  templates in the relevant [asm198x](https://github.com/asm198x) repo.
- **Documentation** — the [docs](https://github.com/asm198x/docs) repo covers
  the ISA spec format and per-machine dialect references.
- **Security** — see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md); please report privately.

It's a small, early project, so replies may take a little while. Thanks for your
patience.
